Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 12, 2026

Overview

Quran Study is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how our app handles data.

Summary: Quran Study is a privacy-focused educational app. Your voice is processed locally on your device for learning purposes only. No personal data is collected, stored, or shared with us or any third parties.

Data Collection

Quran Study processes data locally on your device and does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

Microphone Access

  • Purpose: Voice recognition for Quran recitation practice
  • Processing: All audio is processed locally on your device using Apple's Speech Recognition framework
  • Storage: Audio is NOT recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere
  • Control: You can revoke microphone access anytime in Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone

Speech Recognition

  • Purpose: Analyze and provide feedback on Arabic recitation
  • Framework: Apple's SFSpeechRecognizer for on-device processing
  • Data: Transcribed text is used temporarily for comparison and feedback only
  • Apple's Policy: Speech recognition may use Apple's servers for processing. See Apple's privacy policy for details on how they handle speech data
  • Control: You can disable speech recognition in Settings → Privacy & Security → Speech Recognition

Offline Content

  • Quran Translation: Bundled offline data from public domain sources (github.com/fawazahmed0/quran-api)
  • Hadith Collections: Bundled offline data from public domain sources (github.com/fawazahmed0/hadith-api)
  • No Tracking: The app works completely offline after installation; no analytics or tracking of any kind

Third-Party Services

Quran Study does NOT use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Third-party tracking
  • Cloud storage for user data
  • Social media integration
  • Crash reporting services

Data Retention

Since no personal data is collected or stored by the app, there is no data retention. All processing happens in real-time on your device and is immediately discarded.

Children's Privacy

Quran Study is safe for all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Your Rights

You have complete control over:

  • Microphone access: Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone → Quran Study
  • Speech recognition access: Settings → Privacy & Security → Speech Recognition → Quran Study
  • All app data: Remains on your device and can be completely deleted by uninstalling the app

Security

Since all processing occurs locally on your device and no data is transmitted to external servers, your data remains secure on your device under your control. We implement security best practices in our code to ensure your device's resources are used safely.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Updates will be reflected on this page and the "Last Updated" date will be revised. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Open Source

This app uses public domain Islamic texts from the following sources:

  • Quran API: github.com/fawazahmed0/quran-api
  • Hadith API: github.com/fawazahmed0/hadith-api
